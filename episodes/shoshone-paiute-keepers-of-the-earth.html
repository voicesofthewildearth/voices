Shoshone-Paiute Keepers of the Earth

March 2024  ·  Jane Fritz

Apple Podcasts Spotify

As you drive south from Mountain Home or north from Elko, miles of seemingly endless fence lines mark cattle ranches and frame the highway. But fences suddenly disappear as you enter the Duck Valley Indian Reservation, home to the Newe and Numa peoples — the Western Shoshone and Paiute.

Apple Podcasts · Spotify · voicesofthewildearth@gmail.com

PO Box 2418, Sandpoint, Idaho  |  A nonprofit, volunteer-led project, 1989–2025.

We honor the Schitsu’umsh (Coeur d’Alene), Nimiipuu (Nez Perce), Ktunaxa (Kootenai), Shoshone-Bannock, and Shoshone-Paiute peoples — past, present, and future — and the lands and waters that hold these stories.
