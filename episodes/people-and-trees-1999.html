PO Box 2418, Sandpoint, Idaho  |  A nonprofit, volunteer-led project, 1989–2025.

We honor the Schitsu’umsh (Coeur d’Alene), Nimiipuu (Nez Perce), Ktunaxa (Kootenai), Shoshone-Bannock, and Shoshone-Paiute peoples — past, present, and future — and the lands and waters that hold these stories.
