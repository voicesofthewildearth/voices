Earth Song Reprisal

August 2025  ·  Shane Sater & Jane Fritz

Listen on Spotify Apple Podcasts YouTube

I arrive long before sunrise in this dry part of western Montana. The mountains are black silhouettes around me. It’s late May, and these traditional lands of the Blackfeet and Salish peoples are now settled: the valley farmed, the river dammed, the hills mined. Even so, the singing continues.

We honor the Schitsu’umsh (Coeur d’Alene), Nimiipuu (Nez Perce), Ktunaxa (Kootenai), Shoshone-Bannock, and Shoshone-Paiute peoples — past, present, and future — and the lands and waters that hold these stories.
